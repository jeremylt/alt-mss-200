West Area Computing Unit¶

The West Area Computing Unit was a group of African American female mathematicians who were responsible for many of the calculations for the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics (NACA), the predecessor National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA). Content related to the contribution of women to NASA was removed from NASA websites in accordance with a purge of all diversity related content.

  • Hidden Figures (2016 movie)

  • Hidden Figures: The American Dream and the Untold Story of the Black Women Mathematicians Who Helped Win the Space Race (2016 book)
