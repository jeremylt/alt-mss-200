West Area Computing Unit
********************************************************************************

.. figure:: ../img/NACA.webp
   :alt: NASA emblem
   :width: 200px

The `West Area Computing Unit <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Area_Computers>`_ was a group of African American female mathematicians who were responsible for many of the calculations for the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics (NACA), the predecessor National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA).
Content related to the contribution of women to NASA was `removed from NASA websites <https://orbitaltoday.com/2025/02/11/nasa-ordered-to-remove-women-in-leadership-mentions-from-websites/>`_ in accordance with a purge of all diversity related content.


Materials
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- `Hidden Figures (2016 movie) <https://www.justwatch.com/us/movie/hidden-figures>`_

- `Hidden Figures: The American Dream and the Untold Story of the Black Women Mathematicians Who Helped Win the Space Race (2016 book) <http://margotleeshetterly.com/hidden-figures-nasas-african-american-computers>`_


Assignment
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Two ways to learn about the West Area Computing Unit are given above.
The book is more historically accurate than the movie, as the content for the movie was adapted to best fit that format.
Select at least one of the sources given above, or an alternative source.
On the social media platform of your choice, make a post or series of posts answering the following questions:

1. What are your overall impressions from the stories?
What sticks with you the most strongly?

2. Explain the risks for the NACA and the space mission if the West Area Computing Unit was not properly staffed and supported.
This question may require additional research.

3. Give an example where leadership could have been more proactive in meeting the needs of the West Area Computing Unit.
Discuss the importance of actively seeking ways to best support your team and community.
